Completing Courses

Click here to see a Show Me How video tutorial.

Courses are made up of learning activities, such as tests, online learning, instructor-led classes, and evaluations. When you have completed all of the learning activities in a course, it will be marked as completed and removed from your to-do list. A record of your course completion will appear on your transcript.

To complete a course:

  1. Click the To Do tab to open your To-Do List.
  2. Click a course's Start button to open the Learning Activities page, which displays the learning activities that make up the course.
  • There is a button next to learning activities that you have not yet completed. If the button reads Locked, you cannot start the learning activity until you have completed the one above it in the list.
  1. Click the button next to the first learning activity to launch it.
  • If the learning activity details page opens, click the button next to the activity name to launch the activity.
  1. Complete all of the learning activities to complete the course.
  • A green check [] next to a learning activity means that you have successfully completed it.

You do not have to complete all of a course's learning activities in a single session. You can leave a course and then come back later to resume where you left off. Keep in mind that you must complete all of the learning activities by 11:59 PM Eastern Time on the course's due date. See Resuming Courses.
