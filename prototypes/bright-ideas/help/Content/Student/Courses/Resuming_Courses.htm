Resuming Courses

You do not have to complete all of a course's learning activities in a single session. You can leave a course and then come back later and resume where you left off. Keep in mind that you must complete all of the learning activities by 11:59 PM on the course's due date.

To resume a course:

  1. Click the To Do tab to open your To-Do List.
  2. Click the Resume button next to the course that you want to resume. This either launches the first available learning activity or opens the Learning Activities page.
  • If the Learning Activities page opens, click the button next to the first available learning activity. This either launches the learning activity or opens the learning activity details page.
  • If the learning activity information page opens, click the button next to the activity name to launch the activity.
  1. Complete all of the learning activities to complete the course.
  • A green check [] next to a learning activity means that you have successfully completed it.
