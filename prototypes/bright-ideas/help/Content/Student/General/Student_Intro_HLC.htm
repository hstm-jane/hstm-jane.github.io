Welcome to the HealthStream Learning Center (HLC)

The HealthStream Learning Center (HLC) is a Web-based learning management system that enables you to take online courses that are assigned to you by your organization for compliance training and continuing education. You may also be able to enroll in elective learning for your professional growth.

Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for logging on to the HLC regularly to complete any assigned learning that you might have. If you hold any professional certifications or discipline-related licenses, you should enter this information into the system. Some courses offer continuing education (CE) credit; having the correct discipline and license information in the system ensures that you will receive the correct CE credit when it is available.

Your organization might define additional responsibilities.
